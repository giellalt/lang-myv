###  Syntactic tags

* @+FMAINV : finite main verb
- oaidná: Son oaidná ollislaš gova. - She sees the whole picture	
* @-FAUXV : infinite auxiliary verb
- sáhte: In sáhte gáhku borrat. - I cannot eat cake.
